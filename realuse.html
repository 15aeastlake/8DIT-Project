Microcontrollers can be seen being used in many consumer electronics that members of the public use in their day to day lives! In fact, it is most likely that you yourself have used some gadgets today that have microcontrollers within them. This is due to their specifications that allow them to help tasks to be completed efficiently and cheaply. The small size and limited processing power make them ideal for electronics that do not need to do anything very complicated, such as:

  • Calculators
  • TV Remotes
  • Thermostats
  • Automatic Teller Machines (ATMs)
  • Mobile Phones
